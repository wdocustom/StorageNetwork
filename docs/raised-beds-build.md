# Raised Bed Planter — Build Specification

Construction reference for fence-picket raised bed planters. All boxes are built
from 5/8" × 5-1/2" × 6' cedar/spruce fence pickets (nominal — actual stock is
roughly 5/8" thick × 5-1/2" wide).

## Sides convention

Looking at the planter from a top view, sides are labeled counterclockwise:

- **A** = left short side
- **B** = top long-ish side (or top in a square)
- **C** = right short side
- **D** = bottom

For a square planter (e.g. 24" × 24") all four sides are nominally equal in
overall length, but cut pickets differ between the A/C pair and the B/D pair
because of how the corner legs stack (see below).

## Corner legs (L-profile)

Each corner is an L made from two ripped picket strips:

| Piece | Width | Thickness | Length | Source |
|-------|-------|-----------|--------|--------|
| Long face | 2-15/16" | 5/8" | = box height | One picket ripped to 2-15/16" |
| Short face | ~2-1/2" | 5/8" | = box height | The offcut from the same rip |

The short (2-1/2") strip is glued and brad-nailed into the **broad face** of
the 2-15/16" strip to form an L. Fasten with **1-1/4" brads, 5 per leg**, run
down the length. The resulting corner reads "mostly even" on both visible
outside faces.

A 16-1/2"-tall planter uses legs cut to 16-1/2" (full box height; legs are
flush top and bottom on ground-level units).

## Side wall pickets

Walls are stacked full-width pickets (no rips) running horizontally. Three
pickets stacked = 3 × 5-1/2" = **16-1/2"** wall height.

Cut lengths are reduced from the overall box dimension so the pickets tuck
inside the leg L's:

| Side | Picket length | Rule |
|------|---------------|------|
| A, C | overall width − 1-1/4" | The picket hides 5/8" behind each adjacent leg's perpendicular face |
| B, D | overall width − 2-1/2" | The picket hides behind both the leg face AND the 5/8" edge of the A/C picket on each end |

**24" × 24" example (16-1/2" tall, ground level):**

- A, C pickets: **22-3/4" × 5-1/2" × 5/8"**, qty 3 each (6 total)
- B, D pickets: **21-1/2" × 5-1/2" × 5/8"**, qty 3 each (6 total)

Fasten side pickets with **1" brads, 6 nails per picket per leg** (12 nails
per picket total). Drive into the L-leg behind, not into the adjacent picket.

## Top rim / ledge

A picture-frame border laid flat on top of the walls. Pickets are ripped down
the middle to **~2-3/4" wide**. Corners are **butted "chase-the-tail" style
(not mitered 45°)** — the long pieces run full length, the short pieces fit
between them.

For a 24" × 24" box:

| Piece | Length | Width | Qty |
|-------|--------|-------|-----|
| Long rim | 24" | 2-3/4" | 2 |
| Short rim | 18-1/2" | 2-3/4" | 2 |

(18-1/2" = 24" − 2 × 2-3/4".)
